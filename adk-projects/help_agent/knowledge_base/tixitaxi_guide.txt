# TixiTaxi Coordination Guide

## What is TixiTaxi?

TixiTaxi (also written Tixi Taxi) is a subsidised, wheelchair-accessible transport
service operated in many Swiss cantons for people with disabilities. It provides
door-to-door transport to medical appointments and care facilities at reduced rates
for IV/EL beneficiaries.

TixiTaxi rides are eligible for SVA transport reimbursement under the
**Taxi und andere Fahrdienste** category on Form 5050.

---

## Booking a TixiTaxi Ride via CareConnect

### When to book
Book at least **48 hours before** the appointment. Last-minute bookings cannot be
guaranteed. For recurring appointments (e.g. weekly physiotherapy), contact TixiTaxi
directly to set up a standing order.

### Step-by-step booking
1. Go to **Ride Planning** in CareConnect.
2. Click **Add Ride** and fill in:
   - Date and time of the appointment
   - Pickup address (your home or another address)
   - Destination (care provider name and address)
   - Appointment type (Physiotherapie, Arztbesuch, etc.)
3. Click **Save**.
4. Once your rides for the period are ready, click **Send to TixiTaxi**.
5. CareConnect formats the ride list as an email and sends it to the
   configured TixiTaxi contact address automatically.
6. TixiTaxi will confirm the booking by phone or email. Wait for confirmation
   before assuming the ride is booked.

### What information TixiTaxi needs
- Passenger name and date of birth
- Pickup address and time
- Destination address
- Wheelchair or mobility aid requirements
- Contact phone number for the day of travel

CareConnect pre-fills name and address from your Profile. Make sure your Profile
is up to date before sending.

---

## Cancelling a TixiTaxi Ride

### Via CareConnect (recommended)
1. Find the ride in the **Ride Planning** list.
2. Click **Cancel Ride**.
3. CareConnect automatically calls the TixiTaxi cancellation line via Twilio.
   The call is placed on your behalf — you do not need to be on the phone.
4. The ride is marked as cancelled in your records.

### Cancellation deadline
Cancel at least **24 hours before** the scheduled pickup. Late cancellations
may incur a no-show fee depending on your canton's TixiTaxi operator.

### If the automatic call fails
If you see an error after clicking Cancel Ride, call TixiTaxi directly using
the number for your canton (see below). Note the cancellation reference number
they give you.

## Receipts and Reimbursement

- TixiTaxi provides a receipt or invoice after each ride.
- Upload the receipt to CareConnect (Invoice Submission) and the agent will
  classify it as **Taxi und andere Fahrdienste** on Form 5050.
- In some cantons, TixiTaxi invoices are billed directly to the SVA — check
  with your SVA office whether you still need to include them on Form 5050.

---

## Recurring Appointments

For appointments that happen on a regular schedule (e.g. every Tuesday for
dialysis), you can:
1. Add all future rides in Ride Planning at once (one ride per date).
2. Send the full list to TixiTaxi in one email via CareConnect.
3. TixiTaxi can set up a standing order for recurring routes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can TixiTaxi accommodate a wheelchair?**
Yes — TixiTaxi vehicles are accessible for wheelchairs. Mention the wheelchair
or any specific mobility aids when booking.

**Can a parent or carer accompany the child?**
Yes, in most cases one accompanying person travels at no additional charge.
Confirm with your local TixiTaxi operator when booking.

**What if TixiTaxi is fully booked?**
If TixiTaxi cannot accommodate the ride, you may need to use a private taxi.
Keep the receipt and submit it under the Taxi category on Form 5050 as normal.

**Does TixiTaxi operate on weekends and public holidays?**
Service varies by canton and operator. Check availability when booking, especially
for weekend appointments.
