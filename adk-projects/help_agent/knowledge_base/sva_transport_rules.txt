# SVA Transport Reimbursement Rules — Form 5050

## What is Form 5050?

SVA Form 5050 (Reisekostenabrechnung) is the monthly transport cost table submitted
to the kantonal SVA Durchführungsstelle by families receiving IV/EL benefits. Each row
records one trip to an approved treatment provider.

---

## Required Fields per Row

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Reisedatum | Date of travel (DD.MM.YYYY) |
| Behandlungsgrund | Reason for the appointment (e.g. Physiotherapie, Arztbesuch) |
| Behandlungsort | Name and location of the care provider |
| Cost column | Exactly one of: Billetpreis OV, Privatauto, or Taxi — never more than one |
| Total | Sum of the one filled cost column |

---

## Transport Categories

### 1. OV — Öffentlicher Verkehr (Public Transport)

- **Eligible**: SBB, BLS, ZVV, PostAuto, tram, bus, S-Bahn tickets
- **Class**: 2nd class only. First-class supplements are not reimbursed.
- **Half-fare / GA**: If you hold a Halbtaxabo or GA, use the price you actually paid
  (i.e. the discounted price). Do not claim the full fare.
- **Amount**: The price printed on the ticket or receipt.
- **Column**: Billetpreis OV 2. Klasse

### 2. Privatauto (Private Car)

- **Rate**: CHF 0.70 per kilometre (SVA fixed rate, 2024).
- **No receipt required**: Enter the number of kilometres driven; the app calculates
  the reimbursable amount automatically (km × 0.70).
- **Route**: Direct route from home to care provider and back. Detours are not covered.
- **Column**: Privatauto
- **Note**: The SVA may request a mileage log (Fahrtenbuch) for audit purposes.
  Keep a simple record of date, route, and km.

### 3. Taxi und andere Fahrdienste (Taxi & Transport Services)

- **Eligible**: Licensed taxi, TixiTaxi (subsidised wheelchair transport), Uber,
  Krankenfahrten, Begleitfahrten.
- **Amount**: The amount shown on the receipt or invoice from the taxi company.
- **Receipt required**: Always obtain and keep the original receipt.
- **Column**: Taxi und andere Fahrdienste
- **TixiTaxi note**: TixiTaxi invoices are usually sent separately by the canton.
  Check with your SVA office whether you submit them on Form 5050 or separately.

---

## Rules You Must Follow

1. **One cost column per row.** Never fill two columns for the same trip.
2. **One row per receipt.** Do not combine multiple trips on one row.
3. **Original receipts.** Keep all originals for at least 5 years. The SVA can
   request them during an audit.
4. **Approved providers only.** Reimbursement applies only to trips to care
   providers listed in your IV/EL decision (Verfügung). Contact your SVA office
   if you are unsure whether a new provider is approved.
5. **Monthly submission.** Submit Form 5050 by the end of the month following
   the month of treatment. Late submissions may be rejected.

---

## What Is NOT Covered

- First-class train fares
- Fuel costs claimed separately from the per-km rate
- Parking fees (unless explicitly approved in your Verfügung)
- Transport to appointments not listed in the IV/EL decision
- Travel outside Switzerland (unless approved in advance)
- Meals purchased during travel (these are covered under EL Mahlzeiten, not Form 5050)

---

## Meal Expenses (EL — Ergänzungsleistungen)

Meal costs for a disabled person at an approved day structure or care facility are
reimbursed under EL, not IV transport. They are tracked separately in CareConnect
under the **Meal** category and submitted on the relevant EL claim form, not Form 5050.

---

## Submission Process

1. Collect all receipts for the month.
2. Use CareConnect to upload and log each receipt — the agent assembles each Form 5050 row.
3. Print or export the completed Form 5050 from the app (or your SVA office portal).
4. Attach original receipts and submit to your kantonal SVA Durchführungsstelle
   — by post or via the SVA online portal if available in your canton.
